# Thrissur Pooram Simulator

### Team Name: Rizzlers

- Member 2: Amruth Krishna K - Jyothi Engineering College

### Project Description
Ever felt the urge to hear Pooram melam at 3AM in your room?
We made a simulator that brings the chaotic joy of Thrissur Pooram straight to your browser — no elephants, just vibes.
Totally unnecessary, but kind of addictive.

### The Problem (that doesn't exist)
You’re sitting at home, missing the chaos, rhythm, and sheer loudness of Thrissur Pooram melam. What if you could recreate that… on demand… for absolutely no reason?

### The Solution (that nobody asked for)
We made a browser-based Pooram Simulator that plays different melams at the click of a button. Want to vibe at midnight? Now you can. Loud, unnecessary, and oddly satisfying.
